Instructions

  • To move phyton, player can use arrow keys or “WASD”
  • North with up arrow or ‘w’
  • South with down arrow or ‘s’
  • East with right arrow or ‘d’
  • West with left arrow or ‘a’
  • Phyton’s starting point is in the middle of board and moving east (from left to right) automatically 4 grid per second. The phyton moves only north, south, east, or west.
  • If phyton head hit board edge, then its head should appear on the opposite edge.
  • Player can cancel the rewind feature using “Cancel Rewind” button.
